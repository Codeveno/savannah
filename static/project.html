About Our Project

Tracksavannah is an innovative AI-powered wildlife protection system designed to combat poaching and habitat destruction in conservation areas. Our solution leverages cutting-edge deep learning technology to monitor protected ecosystems in real-time and automatically detect threats to wildlife.

The Challenge We Address

Wildlife populations face unprecedented threats from:

  • Illegal poaching activities
  • Unauthorized human encroachment
  • Habitat destruction
  • Forest fires
  • Suspicious vehicle movements

Our Solution

Tracksavannah provides a comprehensive monitoring system that combines AI technology with conservation needs.

Real-Time Detection

Identifies animals, humans, vehicles, and fires using YOLOv8 object detection, operating 24/7 under various conditions.

Immediate Alert System

Automatic notifications to park rangers with GPS coordinates and classification of threat type.
