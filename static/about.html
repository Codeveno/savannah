#Our Innovation

The Tracksavannah Wildlife Monitoring System

Revolutionizing Wildlife Conservation Through AI

Tracksavannah is an advanced computer vision system designed to monitor wildlife populations and detect anomalies in their behavior patterns. Our platform combines cutting-edge YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection with sophisticated analytics to provide real-time insights into animal movements and behaviors.

AI-Powered Detection

Utilizing deep learning algorithms to accurately identify and classify various wildlife species with over 90% accuracy, even in challenging savannah environments.

Anomaly Detection

Our system automatically flags unusual animal behaviors, unexpected movements, or potential threats, enabling rapid response from conservation teams.

Geospatial Tracking

Integrated mapping technology visualizes animal movements and identifies critical habitat areas needing protection.

Developed under supervision of Prof. MD Trizah, SIST, Kisii University
